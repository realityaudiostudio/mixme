Mix ME

Happy Streaming...

It was a long dream for Reality Audio Studio team to get their own streaming web app Mix ME to public. Reality Audio Studio creates the new dimensions of music and tries to share all over the world. As part of this, we planned to stream music from own settings and minimal designs, we completley switched to our new platform,Mix ME. And this will be the official site for Reality Audio Studio. Also we are planning to stream musics by other small scale talented musicians for free using this platform. This will encourage musicians to enhance them in better manner.

Why Web app?

It was our desire to create not just a website , but an app with a nice interface. We tried making android apps. But unfortunatley failed to publish on google play store. So we created app using HTML,CSS,javascript and hosted using github and made settings to install web app. Advantage is that space of your phone will be consumed in very less manner and also to enjoy from cross platforms.

What about Artists?

We encourage new artists to share their original music content through our app so that they will get an oppurtunity to listen songs in high quality. We will soon add details about artist sharing.

Who Debveloped Mix ME?

This web app is designed from scratch. And completley developed By Alan Jose Santo, Music Producer at Reality Audio Studio. And used Visual Studio Code to develop this. Also used code snippets from W3 Schools and redesigned it.
